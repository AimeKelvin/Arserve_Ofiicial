about us

Arserve multiservice company.

Has been created as a solution to modern event organisation and execution, I have always been passionate about Hospitality and throughout my career and academic life, I observed and came to realize that modern events was lacking a touch from a proffesional to be successful. That's how the idea came along.

We aim at providing Profesional excellent services in Events with a touch of our passion for hospitality starting from small and private to Medium and Large types of event and let our clients know that they can relax and Let us to it for them.
